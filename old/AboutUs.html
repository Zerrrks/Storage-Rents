About Us

Who are we?
We are a group of guys who met at the University of Cincinnati and wanted to create something that we had never seen done before.
Why and when was Storage Rents founded?
Storage Rents was founded in the Fall of 2021 to offer a cheaper solution for external storage compared to storage units for people who do not need all of the space of a storage unit and don't want to spend a lot of money for it.
